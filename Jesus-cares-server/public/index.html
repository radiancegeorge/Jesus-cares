ABOUT JESUS CARES CHARITY

Jesus Cares is a non-profit organization with a mission to build wholesome men from the needy, vulnerable and less-privileged of the society, who will be empowered to become the best possible persons they can be and ultimately live their best lives. These are people whose lives have lost taste and a sense of purpose or direction. However, through our charity and caregiving initiatives, feeding programs, healthcare and medical outreaches, skill acquisition and entrepreneurship programs, seminar and educational campaigns, life empowerment and coaching, counselling and psychosocial support, we are poised to reach out to the broken, lost and vulnerable of the society (whether young or old) whose lives will be thoroughly furnished to regain soundness in spirit, soul and body. Our humanitarian and social entrepreneurship programs are holistic initiatives anchored on three main drivers: The Gospel, Medicine and Education.
